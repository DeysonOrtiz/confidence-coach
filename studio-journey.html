The Studio That
Taught Me Something New

How building Jill's dream studio
reminded me that growth never stops.

I have spent over 30 years behind the camera.

Editing. Animating. Designing.
A quiet craftsman in the glow of monitors and timelines.

So when a client asked me to build her studio, start to finish, it would have been easy to say,
"That is not what I do."

But something in me said,
"Why not?"

I have worked in studios. Lit sets. Held cameras.
I have learned from masters of the craft, gracious people who let me stand beside them and absorb what could not be taught in textbooks.

Still, this was different.

This time, I was the one trusted to make it all happen.
Not just the lights and the mics and the cameras…
But to help someone show up fully, on screen, and in spirit.
